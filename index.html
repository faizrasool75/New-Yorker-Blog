Is this israel's forever war?

The 9/11 attacks and the wars that followed fundamentally rearranged the American national-security apparatus, destabilized the Middle East, and left lasting scars on the American body politic. Foreign-policy analysts whose careers were shaped by the war on terror see troubling parallels with Israel’s war on Gaza, Keith Gessen writes.
